UNITY (Relational Data Base) - home page

UNITY Is a collection of utilities and libraries for manipulating Relational Data Bases stored in flat file format. Due to it's low overhead and ease of use, it's quite popular within Lucent Technologies for a variety of different tasks. It is available under the GNU General Public License.
